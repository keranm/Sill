Sill 0.3.5 brings your day to the sidebar — before you've clicked anything.

  • Heads-up favorites: flip one switch in Settings (⌘,) and your favorites start telling you what's waiting — an unread count on Gmail, pending updates on Home Assistant, and a small card when a Google Calendar meeting is about to start. Click the card and you land on that meeting, details open. Sill reads this from your own signed-in pages, right on your Mac — no accounts to connect, and nothing sent anywhere, ever. Off by default: your mail is yours.
  • Favorites, always ready: your favorite sites now load when Sill opens and stay loaded — clicking one is instant, from any workspace. Favoriting an open tab keeps the page exactly as it was, too.
  • Default browser, properly: Sill now shows up in System Settings › Desktop & Dock as a choice for your default browser.
